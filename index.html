Welcome to MentorMe!

MentorMe is a site created to find a mentor who matches with your academic interests as well as personality. Click the elegibility tab to get started

Mission Statement

Nagivating through one's teenage years has always been difficult, and in today's world of rising expectations and standards some may say it is even impossible. Not all high school students are fortunate enough to have someone willing or capable of giving them the guidance and support necessary to push them to reach their fullest potential as well as steer them in the right path. MentorMe was created to help these teens by creating connections to help them succeed as well-rounded students and individuals
