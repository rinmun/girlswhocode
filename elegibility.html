Why is MentorMe not available for everyone?

MentorMe is not yet available to everyone for two reasons

- The program is focused on those who truly need it, meaning highschool students who do not have the money, resources, or connections to seek help elsewhere

- We are a non-profit organization and have limited funds and staff


What are the eligibility requirements?

There are exceptions, but the general guidelines are

-Family income of under 70k

-Location/available resources

-Other proof of need

-Academic record

-Personal statement
